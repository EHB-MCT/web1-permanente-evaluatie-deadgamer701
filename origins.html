Power

to activate power, you must activate the generator in the area.
There are 6 Generators around the whole map.
to activate the genrators you start the generator and stay on its platform until it has been fully activated.
if you go off the platform the generator starting procedure will stop and slowly go down until it has reached 0 percent,
where it will stop and you need to spend points again.

Pack a Punch

the Pack a Punch machine is in the middle of the map at the excavation site.
to use the machine, you must have all 6 Generators activated.

Challenges/trials

when you start the game you will get 4 challenges with each of them a reward

  • headshot 115 zombies :pack a punch STG-44 or Scar-H
  • activate 6 generators: Max ammo

Collectables

One Inch Punch melee

to get the one inch punch you must first fill all four boxes in the footprints of the middle robot,
and afterwards go to the rewards box either at spawn or at the church and get melee upgrade.
the upgrade makes a melee attack a 1-hit kill untill round 14, if you have a upgraded staff and melee with the staff,
its a 1-hit kill until round 19. later this can also be upgraded to iron fist when completing the easter egg, and will be 1-hit kill
until round 36 and it will have an elemental effect depending on wich staff you have equipped when acquiring iron fist.

Shovel

to get a shovel you can find 2 of them inside spawn the other shovel locations are:

  • Lying in the church, at the rear of the tank near the tank's treads.
  • In the tunnel leading to the wind tunnel, next to Generator Station 4.
  • In the tunnel leading to the lightning tunnel, next to Generator Station 5.
golden shovel

the golden shovel can be obtaineed by digging up 30 spots.
the golden shovel has better loot drop chance aswell unlock other Collectables

Golden helmet

to get the Golden helmet you must first obtain the golden shovel.
after this when you dig a spot up you get the chance of getting the golden helmet.
the golden helmets prevents you from getting downed when a giant robot steps on you.

extra perk slot

to get extra perk slots so you can have more perks, you need to get the golden shovel.
after this next time you get a zombie blood go searching for a orange glowing dig spot and dig it up.
here you get the change to get an empty perk bottle you can use to purchase extra perks.
