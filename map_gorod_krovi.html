Power

Pack a Punch

To activate Pack-a-Punch you must first turn on the power.
After that you must obtain The Dragon Network Controller which is a buildable which is split into 3 parts.
To obtain the Dragon Network Controller you must obtain a cylinder for the following Groph Modules,
activate them/call them in, defend them and eventually they will open to reveal the part:

  • Dragon Control(blue cylinder)
  • Tank Station(red cylinder)
  • Supply Depot(yellow cylinder)

Once you do all three and obtain the Dragon Network Controller parts, you must take them to Operations Bunker and put them into the machine.
You can now ride the Dragon to Pack-a-Punch for 500 points or call in Groph Modules for power-ups.

Challenges/trials

In Spawn there are 4 grave stones (one for each player) with 4 medals.
Each represents a challenge which can be completed for a reward.
You will only get one challenge per medal but they are different every game.
here are all possible challenges listed:

  • Stand in fire for 45 seconds
  • Shoot all arms off of 6 Valkyrie Drones
  • Shoot the camera off of 6 Valkyrie Drones
  • Use all 3 Dragons
  • Get burned by fire and survive
  • Get 100 kills with the MG-42 Turret
  • Shoot the arm off of 5 Mangler's
  • Shoot the helmet off of 5 Mangler's
  • 10 Headshots on Napalm Zombies
  • 10 Headshots on Electric Zombies
  • 100 Kills with Dragon Strikes
  • Kill 8 zombies simultaneously using the elevator
rewards
  • Left Medal Reward: One of the following weapons Pack-a-Punch'd: *FFAR, MX Garand, NX Shadowclaw, L4 Siege or HG-40.
  • Top/Middle Medal Reward: Max Ammo power-up.
  • Right Medal Reward: A perk bottle which gives you a 5th perk slot.

Dragons

wonder weapon: raygun mark 3

The Wonder Weapon on Gorod Krovi is the GKZ-45 MK3 aka Raygun Mark 3.
This is a dual wield weapon which has 3 different types of shots.

The left weapon shoots a yellow ball which slows down Zombies.
The right weapon shoots a high power, concentrated Raygun blast.
If you shoot the Raygun blast into the yellow ball, they will combine to create a Gersch/Apothicon Servant like portal which kills/absorbs Zombies.

Buildables

Dragon Shield

The Dragon Shield is a buildable which protects the back of a player, stopping Zombies from being able to attack them.
It can also be equipped allowing the user to cover their front and to attack the Zombies with it.
the Dragon Shield also has a special ability where you can shoot a green fireball that does a lot of damage.

to obtain the dragon shield you must find the 3 parts scattered around the map and assemble it at a buildable table.
the parts can be found at:

  • head piece

  • heart piece

  • mouth piece

Upgraded Dragon Shield

The Upgraded Dragon shield increases the health of the shield, the damage and range it does and also changes the front eyes from green to red.
In order to upgrade the Dragon Shield, you must first get around 50 kills with your regular Dragon Shield. You will then be required to locate 3 lines of Apothicon symbols around the map:

Once you locate them, simply shoot them all with your regular shield (you should be able to do this in one take since you have 3 shots with a shield) and then they will disappear.
After you do that, go back to Spawn where the challenges/trials grave stones are and look into the opened up dead Dragon corpus You will get a pop up that says "Press Button" to Upgrade Shield",
press that, your shield will go in the pool of blood and come out Upgraded.

Unlockables

Gauntlet Of Siegfried

The Gauntlet of Siegfried is the special weapon of Gorod Krovi. It has 3 different attacks:

  • The Dragon Flamethrower which burns/kills Zombies.
  • The Flying Dragon which shoots fireballs at Zombies.
  • The 115 Punch which lets your fly through a horde of Zombies in one punch.
How to obtain
  1. Go to the Pack-a-Punch/Lockdown area and obtain the Dragon Egg in the lower areas, it can be seen on the roof above the sewer system exit, simply shoot it and it will fall down.
  2. Locate a nest around the map and place in the Egg, have a Dragon breathe fire on it and let it cool for 1 or 2 rounds.
  3. Complete all tasks for the Dragon Egg in this order: Napalm Kills, Penetrating Multikills & Melee Kills.
  4. Go to the Pack-a-Punch/Lockdown area and Incubate your egg in the bunker, To incubate the eggs you simply place them under the bunker in one of the 4 machines and charge them by killing zombies near it.
  5. Let the egg cool for 1 or 2 rounds and then go back to spawn and receive the Gauntlet of Siegfried from your challenges/trials grave stone.

Dragon Strikes

The Dragon Strikes are a weapon that is achieved by completing one of the Lockdown modes in Gorod Krovi.

Mangler Helmet

This helmet can be achieved by killing numerous Mangler's! It gives you 50% less damage taken from Mangler's and 30% increased damage when attacking Mangler's.
It can be located on the second floor of the Department store. It will replace the Valkyrie Helmet or Dragon Wings if you have either equipped.

Valkyrie Helmet

This helmet can be achieved by shooting the eye off lots of Valkyrie Drones! It gives you 50% less damage taken from Valkyries and 30% increased damage when attacking Valkyrie Drones.
It can be located on the second floor of the Department store. It will replace the Mangler Helmet or Dragon Wings if you have either equipped.

Dragon Wings

These wings can be achieved by riding the dragon at all 3 platforms and obtaining the Gauntlet of Siegfried!
It gives you 30% less fire & explosive damages, makes the Dragon cost free and allows you to teleport to Pack-a-Punch instantly!
It can be located on the second floor of the Department store.
It will replace the Valkyrie Helmet or Mangler Helmet if you have either equipped.
